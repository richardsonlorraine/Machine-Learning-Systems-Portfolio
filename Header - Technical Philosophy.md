Technical Philosophy: 

The projects within this portfolio are guided by five core engineering principles:

* Reproducibility: Systems should be deterministic, version-controlled, and capable of being recreated reliably.
* Scalability: Architectures should support increasing workloads through modular and distributed design.
* Observability: Operational systems must provide visibility into behaviour, performance, and failure conditions.
* Security: Security considerations should be incorporated throughout the development lifecycle.
* Maintainability: Solutions should remain understandable, testable, and extensible over time.

These principles inform all design decisions presented throughout the portfolio.
